📍 Delivery Area

We currently deliver only within our defined delivery zones. You can check if your location is serviceable directly on our website by entering your address or using the "Use My Location" option.

Orders placed from outside the serviceable area will not be accepted. We are actively working to expand our delivery zones.

⏰ Delivery Time

🌅 All deliveries are made by 10:00 AM every morning — so your puja starts fresh and on time.
  • Deliveries begin early morning and are completed by 10:00 AM.
  • Exact delivery time within that window depends on the route and number of orders.
  • If you are not available, the flowers will be left at your door.

🚫 Delivery Suspension — Force Majeure

Riddhi Siddhi Florals reserves the right to suspend or delay delivery on any day due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control. These include:

  • Government Restrictions: Curfew, Section 144, area lockdowns, or any government order restricting movement in our delivery zone
  • Heavy Rainfall: Extreme weather making delivery unsafe or operationally impossible
  • Natural Disasters: Floods, earthquakes, storms, or other natural calamities
  • Public Unrest: Bandh, riots, protests, or civil disturbances affecting our delivery routes
  • Declared Holidays: National or state holidays declared at short notice that impact supply or logistics
  • Supplier Disruption: Non-availability of fresh flowers at the market due to any external reason
⚠️ No refund or compensation will be provided for deliveries suspended due to the above reasons. We will make every effort to inform affected customers in advance via WhatsApp or phone call.

For subscription customers, missed days due to force majeure will not be added to the subscription period or compensated.
